Early Life

Donald John Trump was born on June 14, 1946, in Queens, New York City. He was the fourth of five children in the Trump family.

Business Career

Before entering politics, Trump was a businessman and television personality. He ran the Trump Organization and hosted *The Apprentice*.

Presidency

Donald Trump served as the 45th president of the United States from 2017 to 2021. His presidency focused on "America First" policies.
